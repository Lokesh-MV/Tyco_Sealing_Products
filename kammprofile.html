Kammprofile Gaskets

Kammprofile gaskets, also known as Camprofile gaskets or serrated gaskets, are a type of semi-metallic gasket commonly used in various industries for high-temperature and high-pressure applications. Kammprofile gaskets are suitable for flanged connections where a tight and leak-free seal is required.

  • Kammprofile gaskets have a solid metal core with concentric serrations or ridges on both sides. These serrations increase the gasket's flexibility and allow it to conform to irregular flange surfaces, creating multiple sealing points.

  • The solid metal core of the gasket provides strength and rigidity, ensuring the gasket maintains its shape and integrity during installation and operation.

  • To enhance sealing performance, Kammprofile gaskets often have a layer of flexible filler material, such as graphite, PTFE, or other suitable materials, sandwiched between the metal core and the flange faces.

  • Kammprofile gaskets can be manufactured from various metal materials, including stainless steel, carbon steel, and other alloys, allowing them to be used in a wide range of applications.
