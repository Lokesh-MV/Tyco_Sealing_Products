Non-Asbestos Gaskets

As a responsible manufacturer, we prioritize the safety and health of our customers and the environment. Our Non-Asbestos Gaskets are made from premium non-toxic materials without compromising on performance. These gaskets are not only reliable but also environmentally friendly.

Compressed Non-Asbestos Fiber gasket is designed to withstand the robust standards of performance for a varied range of industrial applications providing a long-term sealing solution even under the most rugged operating conditions.

Reinforced Graphite Gaskets

Reinforced graphite gaskets are specialized sealing components that combine layers of graphite material with reinforcing materials to enhance their sealing properties. These gaskets are designed to provide reliable and effective sealing solutions, particularly in high-temperature and high-pressure applications.

Features of Reinforced Graphite Gaskets

  • Reinforced graphite gaskets offer superior sealing performance, effectively preventing leaks compared to standard graphite gaskets.

  • They consist of multiple layers of graphite sheets or foils interleaved with reinforcing materials like stainless steel or metals.

  • Can withstand extreme temperatures, making them ideal for heat-exposed applications.

  • Highly adaptable, conforming to irregular surfaces and providing secure seals.

  • Like standard graphite, they resist adhesion, ensuring gasket integrity.

  • Excel in resisting various chemicals and corrosive substances, suitable for aggressive environments.

  • Tailored for specific applications, ensuring precise and secure sealing solutions.

Armed Fiber Gaskets

Aramid fibre gaskets are a versatile and durable sealing solution that offer exceptional performance in challenging applications. Manufactured from high-grade aramid fibres, these gaskets possess unique properties that make them ideal for use in various industries.

Features of Armed Fiber Gaskets

  • Exceptional sealing performance

  • Enhanced torque retention

  • Significant reduction in emissions

  • Cost savings achieved by minimizing waste, maintenance, inventory, fluid loss, and energy consumption

  • Offered in both sheet and custom-cut gasket formats
