PTFE Gaskets

A PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) gasket is a type of gasket made from PTFE material, PTFE is a synthetic fluoropolymer known for its excellent chemical resistance, low friction, and non-stick properties. It is often used in gasket applications to enhance the performance and sealing capabilities of the gasket.
