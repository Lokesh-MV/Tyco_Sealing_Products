Our Products

Explore our comprehensive selection of top-tier industrial gaskets

Non-Metallic Gaskets

Non-metallic gaskets are versatile sealing solutions used across industries. Made from materials like rubber and PTFE, they offer excellent sealing in diverse conditions. These gaskets are prized for their chemical resistance, adaptability, and temperature range, making them vital in chemical processing, HVAC, electrical equipment, and more. They provide leak-free seals in pipes, valves, and electrical enclosures, ensuring safety, efficiency, and compliance with regulatory standards in various industrial applications.

Rubber Gaskets

This joint has an oval shape and is completely covered in nitrile rubber. The ring-type joint material is typically made of low carbon steel or soft iron. It is used in pressure testing to protect the flange surfaces while also providing additional seals.

PTFE Gaskets

A PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) gasket is a type of gasket made from PTFE material, PTFE is a synthetic fluoropolymer known for its excellent chemical resistance, low friction, and non-stick properties. It is often used in gasket applications to enhance the performance and sealing capabilities of the gasket.

Expanded PTFE Gaskets

Expanded PTFE gaskets, derived from PTFE, offer superior chemical resistance, non-stick properties, and high-temperature tolerance for reliable, chemically resistant seals in various industrial applications, overcoming traditional PTFE gasket limitations.

Non-Asbestos Gaskets

Non-asbestos gaskets are vital in the oil and gas industry and all the Industrial applications for safety compliance, superior sealing, versatility, durability, cost-efficiency, and environmental friendliness, ensuring reliable operations in demanding conditions.
