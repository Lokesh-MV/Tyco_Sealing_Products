Expanded PTFE Gaskets

Expanded PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) gaskets are specialized sealing products used in a wide range of industrial applications where a reliable and chemically resistant seal is required. PTFE is a synthetic fluoropolymer known for its exceptional chemical resistance, non-stick properties, and high-temperature tolerance. Expanded PTFE gaskets are derived from PTFE and are designed to overcome some of the limitations of traditional PTFE gaskets.

Features of Expanded PTFE Gaskets

  • Resistant to chemicals, acids, bases, and solvents, ideal for corrosive environments.

  • Withstands extreme temperatures without losing sealing capabilities.

  • Creates reliable seals, conforms to irregular surfaces, suitable for varying pressures and temperatures.

  • Highly adaptable, conforms to different surfaces, reduces leak risks.

  • Resistant to substance adhesion, ideal for low-maintenance applications.

  • Suited for diverse industries (chemical, pharmaceutical, food, power) in various equipment.

  • Requires lower bolt loads, simplifying installation and reducing equipment damage risks.

  • Maintains shape and seal integrity over time, even under prolonged compression.

  • Available as sheets, tapes, and custom-cut shapes for specific sealing needs.
