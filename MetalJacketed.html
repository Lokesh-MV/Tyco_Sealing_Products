Metal-Jacketed Gaskets

Metal jacketed gaskets are a versatile sealing solution that offers superior performance in demanding environments. These gaskets consist of a metallic outer shell, typically made of materials like Monel, steel, brass, or aluminum, and a compressible insert, commonly made of graphite or other materials upon request. The combination of the metallic jacket and the insert material allows metal jacketed gaskets to withstand extreme temperatures and pressures, providing a reliable seal that prevents leaks and ensures the integrity of the system.

Tyco Sealing Products

Types of Metal Jacketed gasket:

  • Corrugated Metal Jacketed Gaskets: These gaskets have a corrugated or grooved metal jacket, providing excellent flexibility and conformability.

  • Flat Metal Jacketed Gaskets: Flat metal-jacketed gaskets have a smooth surface without corrugations.

  • Double-Jacketed Gaskets: These gaskets have two layers of metal jackets, often with a soft filler material between them.

  • Heat Exchanger Gaskets: Metal-jacketed gaskets designed specifically for heat exchangers.
