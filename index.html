About Us

Tyco Sealing Products is a leading manufacturer and supplier of metallic seals and gaskets . With a strong focus on quality and innovation, the company has established itself as a trusted provider of sealing solutions for various industries. From aerospace and automotive to oil and gas, Tyco Sealing Products offers a wide range of products that are designed to meet the unique needs of each application.

Our well-equipped production facility ensures top-quality products. Tyco Sealing Products specializes in Ring Type Joint Gaskets, Spiral Wound Gaskets, Non-Asbestos Gaskets, Pure Silver Gaskets, Fasteners, Bolts, Valve Seat Rings, and Seal Rings with professional expertise.

Who we are

Exploration and extraction of oil from on shore and off shore rigs demand high degree of process efficiency. Sealing plays a very crucial part in this process.

At Tyco Sealing Products, we understand the importance of reliability and durability in industrial applications.

Our diverse range, including Ring Type Joint Gaskets, Spiral Wound Gaskets, and more, demonstrates expertise. Precision-engineered with cutting-edge technology for superior performance.
