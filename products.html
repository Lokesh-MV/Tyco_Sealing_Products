RTJ Gaskets

At Tyco sealing we manufacture different types of RTJs, our RTJs excel in providing a tight seal that can handle the most extreme conditions. Trust in our gaskets to keep your operations running smoothly and securely

VX-AX Gaskets

VX gaskets excel in tough conditions, vital in oil and gas for wellheads and riser connectors. Face-to-face pressure design ensures secure sealing. AX gaskets prevent galvanic corrosion and offer electrical resistance.

IX Gaskets

IX gaskets (IX 15 to IX 1200) adhere to NORSOK L005 standards, using NORSOK M650/M630 materials with PTFE/XYLON coating and color coding for material identification. Custom options, including silver coating, are available.

Spiral Wound Gaskets

Engineered with precision, our Spiral Wound Gaskets provide outstanding resilience and flexibility. Their unique design allows for excellent compression and recovery properties, ensuring a tight and reliable seal in any circumstance.

Kammprofile Gaskets

Kammprofile gaskets, also called Camprofile or serrated gaskets, are semi-metallic gaskets used in high-temperature, high-pressure settings. Ideal for tight, leak-free seals in various industries such as Oil and Gas Industries.

Metal Jacketed Gaskets

Metal jacketed gaskets feature a durable metallic outer shell (e.g., Monel, steel) and a compressible insert (e.g., graphite). They excel in extreme conditions, maintaining system integrity with reliable sealing for all the Industrial usage.

Non Metallic Gaskets

Modified PTFE gaskets, while susceptible to creep under heavy loads, excel as chemically inert gasket materials. They blend PTFE's inertness with stress retention, mitigating its impact on mechanical stability in joints for high-strength, soft gaskets.

High Tensile Fasteners

Tyco Sealing Products excels in crafting high-grade stainless steel fasteners, tailored to client specs and drawings. We prioritize top-quality production, adhering to very strict and appropriate SAE and ASTM size standards.
